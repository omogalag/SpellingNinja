Spelling Ninja

App to learn and practice spelling in English.

1. Learn the alphabet sounds

Press the button to see a large letter and its adapted transcription below.

Aa
/ei/
Look at the letter and repeat the adapted transcription.

2. Alphabet flip cards

Click each card to see the adapted pronunciation on the back.

3. Word generator by level

Choose a level and generate a word. When you flip the card, you will see the spelling transcription based on its letters.

Click the card to flip between the word and the spelling.
